Social Evening

We invite you to join our conference Social Evening & Dinner on Wednesday, September 4. located at Loď Pivovar (‘The Ship Brewery’). Traditional Czech cuisine and microbrewery situated on the anchored boat on river Vltava are combined to offer a unique experience. The guests will have a chance to experience a Brewery Tour from the Headbrewer. The boat is anchored and will not be departing, so you can join us later if needed.

The venue capacity is limited to 80 seats. Tickets are still available, secure your place and don’t miss the opportunity of joining the celebrations. Social dinner (€ 50) is not included in the registration fee.
