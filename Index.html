    About

    www.icdvrat.com

    In addition to ICDVRAT’s usual topics listed below this edition will have an additional focus on AI applications of immersive technologies.

    Conference topics

    Virtual, Augmented Reality, and Mixed Reality environments | Physical rehabilitation | Cognitive rehabilitation | Clinical assessment | Clinical intervention | AI | Machine learning | Affective computing | Synthetic Agents | Input devices | Remote/Telecare | Game Based Learning | Serious Games | VR for Health | Sensory impairment | Mobile health applications | Robotics and education | Rehabilitation robotics | Communications aids | Communication and language

    Cancellation policy: Registrations are only refundable after 25% administrative deduction and no refunds will be made if within 4 weeks of the conference. Delinquent payment will result in cancelling registration. In the case of natural events that may cause mass cancellation, registration fees will not be refunded.
