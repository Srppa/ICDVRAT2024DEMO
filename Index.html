    Specials

    Cyberspace, Behavior and e-Therapy (CYBER) sessions

    ICDVRAT Program Day 1 - September 3, 2024

    The Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Cyberspace, Behavior and e-Therapy (CYBER) is a unique interdisciplinary program that combines behavioral sciences with digital technology. Offered by Universidade Lusófona, Universitat de Barcelona, and Université Paris Cité, this innovative program includes courses at each participating university and internships at more than 30 associated global partners. Students from diverse academic backgrounds are paired to foster a collaborative learning environment focused on applying digital processes and systems to impact human behavior. CYBER not only equips students with technical and theoretical knowledge but also emphasizes practical application and real-world impact, making it an ideal for those aiming to lead in the fields of digital behavior intervention. You can find more about cyber program at https://www.cyber-t.eu/
